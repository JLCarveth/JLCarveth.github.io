What Makes Kotlin Concise?

JLCarveth
2017-10-24
Android, Java, Kotlin

When I first started reading about Kotlin, I found many people praising Kotlin for being a concise language. This had me interested. I began reading the article, only to have them gloss over Kotlin’s brevity, going on about something else entirely. So I decided to find out for myself. In this article, I will cover all of Kotlin’s features that allow you to do more at once.

Extension Functions

Conclusion

While these aren’t the only Kotlin features that will clean up and shorten your code, they are some of the more effective methods. There are more ways to shorten your Kotlin code, especially if the context is an Android application, as there is a whole Kotlin library for eliminating some of Android’s boilerplate.
