About Me

I have been writing code for over 10 years, and have experience with a wide array of languages, frameworks, libraries, and services. I have experience working on multi-tier applications, and have used multiple MVC frameworks including Laravel, JavaEE, and .NET. In addition to languages and frameworks, I have also become quite familiar with tools such as Docker, and cloud service providers like Microsoft Azure, DigitalOcean, and Google Cloud Platform.

Some of my spare time is spent making contributions to open source software used by tens of thousands of people. When I am not writing clean code, I am making homemade pizza!
